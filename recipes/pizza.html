Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for a pepperoni pizza

This recipe takes 25 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 16 0z pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18-20 pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees
  2. Make sauce
  3. Roll out dough
  4. Add toppings
  5. Cook pizza
  6. slice and serve
